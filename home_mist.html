Synopsis

Dark and twisted stories

The Noir Case File

A private detective in the 1940s gets a visit from a femme fatale who reminds him of a deceased woman in a case that he couldn't solve.

The Poet in the Attic

A 19th century poet discovers a magical quill that brings the poems he writes to life. With it he attempts to bring back his dead lover.

The Man Who Has Eight

Set in the 1970s. Blair Comfield, an arachnophobic, travels to see an old woman who has a strange house servant all too eager for her to be there.

The Customer

A shop owner in the 1950s has a very rare item for sale: the head of an alien. As she is closing up for the night, a customer comes in who takes an interest in it.
