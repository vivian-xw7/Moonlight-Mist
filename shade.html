Steven Shade

Steve began his ﬁlm studies in 2013 at the University of Kansas. While in the program he was given the chance to learn and work with Kevin Willmott and Matt Jacobson. He worked on a number of short ﬁlms as a student and started making my own in 2014. Since then he’s directed and written his own ﬁlms that are part of an anthology called The Moonlight Mist while running camera, sound, and other various production roles for other ﬁlms, most recently helping with the feature I Am Lisa by Kansas City ﬁlmmaker Patrick Rea.

He started doing videography work in 2017 helping with weddings and then managing his own photo/video team ﬁlming a dance recital that includes editing and producing the dvds which he’s done for 3 years now. He’s done interviews, helped capture concerts, and is looking to expand his experience pushing to new limits.
